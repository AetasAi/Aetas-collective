Partner & Ambassador Directory

The specialist partner network designed to support financial wellbeing and improve Return on Employee Investment. Carefully selected experts delivering targeted, high-quality support — ensuring employers access the right expertise without the complexity of managing multiple providers.

How it works

The Collective Structure

Ambassadors

Senior professionals who support Aetas in the Workplace in a non-commercial, introductory capacity. Trusted voices who introduce the programme to business leaders, share its principles, and open doors to meaningful conversations. Their role is one of advocacy, relationships, and reach.

Partners & Service Providers

Specialist service providers who form the core of the Collective. Each partner is selected for their expertise, quality, and relevance to the Aetas ITW framework — delivering services directly to client organisations across seven core categories.

The Founding Organisation

About Aetas

Aetas Partners and Aetas Wealth are the founding organisations behind Aetas in the Workplace. Together they provide the financial planning expertise, advisory infrastructure, and programme design that sits at the core of the ITW proposition.

Aetas Partners

● Matthew Steiner  ·  aetas-partners.com

The founding organisation behind Aetas in the Workplace, bringing together financial expertise, specialist partner networks, and advisory capability to help employers improve employee financial wellbeing and business performance. Aetas Partners leads the design and delivery of financial education workshops, one-to-one guidance sessions, and wellbeing communications that help employees build financial confidence and make better-informed decisions.

Aetas Wealth

● Daniel Cottam  ·  aetas-wealth.com

The regulated financial planning and wealth management arm of Aetas Partners, operating as a trading style of Insight Financial Associates Limited (FCA registration 458421). Led by Daniel Cottam, Aetas Wealth provides holistic financial planning covering pensions, investment, protection, estate planning, and cashflow modelling — delivering the regulated advice that sits at the heart of the employee financial wellbeing proposition.
